Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee is an English computer scientist who is, more notably, the inventor of the World Wide Web. He is now the director of the W3C. The W3C manages the web, along with ICANN who oversees its progress.

The DNS translates domain names to IP addresses. IP addresses are used when routing packets, which are bits of information, to determine where the packet is coming from and going to. Similar to packets, HTTP and HTTPS transfer data; HTTPS is just more secure than HTTP.

To determine how a website on the internet is styled and the way it will be presented to an internet user, HTML and CSS are utilized. HTML and CSS perform in conjunction, dictating the aesthetics of a webpage.
